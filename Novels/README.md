# KINGDOM OF DESTINY: Solomon's Heritage

This repository serves as a professional portfolio showcasing the narrative architecture, world-building, and quest logic of my published novel, **"Kingdom of Destiny: Solomon's Heritage"** (ISBN: 9798325091551).

---

## 📖 Project Overview
*Solomon's Heritage* is a dual-timeline epic that bridges high-fantasy mythological lore with modern investigative thriller elements. This project demonstrates my ability to manage complex "parallel-quest" structures where historical artifacts serve as keys to contemporary survival.

### The Two Timelines:
1. **The Legend of the Third Heir:** Prince Solomon is chosen by King Orin to become the third heir of magic. He must unite fragmented tribes and lead an army against the dark representative, Valefar.
2. **The Modern "Library":** Centered around a secret base hidden as a city library, modern-day Headknights must recover Solomon’s lost relics to prevent a global dark magic incursion.

## 🗺️ Narrative Design & "Quest" Structure
The novel is structured around different kind of enviroment and systems. Here are five different part from the novel to show variaty.

### 1. The Sahara Excavation (Dungeon Design)
* **Objective:** Retrieve Solomon’s Parchment from one of three identical hidden pyramids in the desert.
* **Logic:** * **Environmental Puzzle:** Locate the correct structure by using a specific gold coin that glows to reveal the entrance beneath the sand.
    * **High-Stakes Escape:** Once the parchment is retrieved from the central wooden box, a "sinking sand" mechanic triggers. The player must platform up collapsing stairs and escape through the roof before the entire structure is buried alive.

### 2. The FBI Base Assault (Heist/Distraction Quest)
* **Objective:** Launch a coordinated fake assault to force the FBI to enact an early emergency transport of Professor Elnore's files.
* **Logic:**
    * **Distraction Tactics:** Coordinate multiple teams to fire grenade launchers at the perimeter walls and launch an EMP to cut the base's power.
    * **Hit-and-Run:** Use motorcycles to quickly extract the assault teams before the base's heavily armed guards and bazooka units can retaliate in the dark.

### 3. Escape from the Crystal Cave (Chase & Hazard Evasion Quest)
* **Objective:** Outrun a magical trap deep within the Everest cave system.
* **Logic:**
    * **Environmental Hazard:** Navigating into a section filled with blue crystals triggers a trap; the crystals turn red, unleashing a massive, wall-to-wall wave of fire.
    * **Chase Sequence:** Sprint back through the winding corridors, managing stamina to outrun the flames until hitting the invisible magical barrier that stops the fire.

### 4. The Palace of the Rock (Dungeon & Exploration Quest)
* **Objective:** Locate and unlock Solomon's hidden underground palace using the retrieved sword.
* **Logic:**
    * **Magical Key Puzzle:** Use the gauntlet's vision to locate the exact spot on a rocky mountain. Plunge the sword into the ground to trigger a localized earthquake, splitting the rock to reveal hidden stairs.
    * **Navigation:** Descend into complete darkness until a magical gust of wind sequentially lights torches down a massive, ancient corridor.

### 5. The Final Stand (Hold the Line & Boss Fight Quest)
* **Objective:** Defeat the giant rebel commander and secure the realm.
* **Logic:**
    * **Magic Awakening:** Play as James as his eyes turn golden, fully awakening his magical potential.
    * **Brutal Duel:** Engage the massive commander in a heavy, punishing melee duel in the mud. Dodge wide, devastating mace swings and counter-attack to shatter his armor and end the war.

## 🛡️ World-Building: Itemization & Magic System
The "Magic System" is blood-locked and artifact-dependent, creating a "Power-Up" progression for characters.

* **The Gauntlet of Coldor:** A narrative utility item that grants "Visions". It requires a blood oath and specific lineage to activate.
* **The Seal of Solomon (Ring):** The primary focus for environmental magic and an item to contol magic.
* **The Book of Solomon:** The "Endgame" artifact. It serves as a vessel to imprison dark entities like Grimor.

# SUMMARY

### CHARACTER ROSTER (NPCs & Protagonists)

#### Protagonist Faction (The Knights)
* **Nathaniel Cooper (Headknight):** Reluctant leader. Struggles with alcoholism and grief. **Special Mechanic:** Wields a magical gauntlet that triggers interactive visions of the past.
* **Rose Anton:** High-tier combat specialist. Daughter of the Library's director.
* **Scatt Flag:** Veteran tactician. Distinctive trait: Has only one arm.
* **Ian James Fate:** Rescued from the magical realm. Possesses critical inherited knowledge about the Book's location. Guide archetype.

#### Antagonist Faction
* **Blacked (Primary Target):** Corrupted rebel magic user. Commands an army of monsters. Seeks the Book for world domination.
* **Christian Sharp (Secondary Target):** Ruthless FBI Agent. Serves as a mortal, bureaucratic, and tactical obstacle.

### QUEST PROGRESSION (Main Storyline)

#### Phase 1: The Parchment Retrieval
* **Location:** Sahara Desert, Egypt
* **Objective:** Retrieve Solomon's Parchment.
* **Events:** 1. Detect magical anomaly.
    2. Arrive at coordinates; three identical, trap-filled pyramids rise from the sand.
    3. Party splits up.
    4. Scatt navigates shifting architecture, secures parchment.
    5. *Environmental Hazard:* Pyramids sink back into the sand immediately after retrieval.

#### Phase 2: The Translation Heist
* **Location:** Modern City / FBI Base
* **Objective:** Translate the unreadable parchment.
* **Events:** 1. Nathaniel uses gauntlet on parchment -> Triggers playable vision of Solomon's past.
    2. Learn translation requires confiscated files of missing explorer (Prof. Dor Elnore).
    3. Files are held by FBI Agent Christian Sharp.
    4. *Attempt 1 (Heist):* Distraction attack on fortified FBI convoy. (Fails/Goes wrong).
    5. *Attempt 2 (Hostage Trade):* Kidnap the newly elected City Mayor. Blackmail Sharp into trading the files for the Mayor.
    6. Files reveal next waypoint: Mount Everest.

#### Phase 3: Crossing the Threshold
* **Location:** Mount Everest / Kingdom of Destiny
* **Objective:** Locate the Book of Magic.
* **Events:** 1. Scale Everest, locate hidden cave network.
    2. Portal transition into the alternate magical realm.
    3. Infiltrate dungeon to rescue Ian James Fate (contains exact coordinates of the Book).
    4. Evasion sequences: Actively hunted by Blacked and his monster army.

#### Phase 4: The Final Battle
* **Location:** Palace Ruins / Battlefield
* **Objective:** Secure or destroy Solomon's Book.
* **Events:** 1. Massive medieval war between Kingdom defenders and Blacked's corrupted rebels.
    2. Party pushes through battlefield to the central pillar/altar.
    3. Blacked reaches the Book first.
    4. *Climax Choice/Action:* Nathaniel and James realize the artifact is too dangerous. Nathaniel throws a magical sword, burning the Book to ash.
    5. *Resolution:* Allied cavalry (King Augustine) arrives, routing dark forces. Blacked escapes.
