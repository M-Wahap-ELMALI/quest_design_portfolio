This year(2026) I found how it should continue so after finishing **The Hope and The Curse** I will continue to write it. For now I am leaving the first draft's overview.

This directory serves as an official repository showcasing the narrative architecture, psychological dark fantasy elements, and core world-building lore of my upcoming contemporary dark fantasy novel, **"Redemption: The Saint and the Beast"**.

---

## Project Overview

*Redemption: The Saint and the Beast* is an atmospheric, gritty urban dark fantasy that explores the fracturing of a man's sanity under the weight of a horrific cosmic curse. The story blurs the line between human guilt, divine judgment, and primordial monstrosity.

The narrative leverages a **dual-reality framing device**. On the surface, the story tracking Adam Herrick feels like a bleak, contemporary crime noir set in the rain-slicked streets of Birmingham, involving loan sharks, debt, and underground crime syndicates. However, when a desperate act of violence accidentally triggers an ancient cosmic entity, the story plunges into a hidden world of occult orders, divine gatekeepers, runic restrictions, and terrifying visions of a forgotten history.

---

## Core Story Threads

* **The Burden of the Beast:** Adam Herrick is a desperate, skinny street criminal drowning in unpayable debts to a brutal underground boss named Wood. After a botched heist leads to the accidental killing of a priest, Adam becomes the unwilling host to an ancient, vengeful entity. Trapped in a cycle of waking amnesia, he must navigate the psychological horror of sharing his flesh with a bloodthirsty beast.

* **The Shield of Brotherhood:** Edward Steveson is Adam's fiercely loyal childhood friend and a craftsman of historical artifacts. Acting as Adam’s moral anchor and grounded confidant, Edward uses his tech-noir smithing skills to build specialized, electric-shock iron restraints to help keep Adam's inner demon contained.

* **The Anchor of Light:** Diana Amnell is the beacon of normalcy and unyielding affection in Adam's spiraling world. Even as Adam attempts to distance himself to protect her from his awakening curse, Diana refuses to abandon him, eventually drawing the dangerous attention of both mundane crime lords and secret arcane organizations.

## World-Building & Thematic Architecture


* **The Prince of Hell & The Broken Seal:** The universe relies on a cosmic system of separation. Centuries ago, humanity's greatest king sacrificed his own soul to forge an ancient helmet, sealing away a ruthless, devouring race known as the *Ejrahs and Marjs*. When occult collectors break the helmet's horns, the energy barrier leaks, weakening the laws of physics and allowing dark princes to slip into human hosts.

* **Iron Sorcery & Runic Alchemy:** In this world, magic operates on rigid physical and geographical costs. Pure iron is a toxic, burning anathema to supernatural entities. To cast protective wards, invoke ancient dialogues, or lock a demon away, characters must draw complex geometric matrices (circles bound by intersecting triangles) using specific conduits like chalk-stone directly onto localized focal points.
